Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Tharses

What type of business is Tharsses?

We operate as a cross-border e-commerce company, specializing in mobile shopping applications. We partner with Chinese manufacturers to source quality goods, manage the logistics, and provide customer service.

How do I download the Tharsses app?

The Tharsses app will be available for download on both the Apple App Store (for iOS devices) and the Google Play Store (for Android devices). Visit either store and search for "Tharsses."

How do I create an account?

You can create an account easily within the app using your phone number or email address.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods, including local mobile money options (like Telebirr), CBE Birr and traditional debit/credit cards. We're committed to providing secure and convenient payment options for all users.

How long does delivery take?

We strive to provide fast and reliable delivery. Delivery times vary depending on your location and the specific product. We offer same-day delivery in Addis Ababa.

Can I order products from Mekelle?

Currently, Tharsess focuses in addis ababa region and will expand to other Ethiopian regions.
