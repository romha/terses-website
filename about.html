About Tharses

Bridging Ethiopia and China through innovative e-commerce

Our Story

Tharsses is a mobile shopping application built in Ethiopia that specializes in cross-border e-commerce between Ethiopia and China. We partner directly with Chinese manufacturers to source high-quality products and offer them to Ethiopian consumers at prices significantly lower than traditional retail.

Our Mission

Our mobile-first approach provides a convenient and accessible shopping experience for everyone. We believe in modernizing the e-commerce industry in Ethiopia by implementing international standards, prioritizing customer satisfaction, and fostering economic development through access to affordable goods.
